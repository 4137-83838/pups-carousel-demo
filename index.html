What are the most popular breeds?

  • Pembroke Welsh Corgi

    Among the most agreeable of all small house dogs, the Pembroke Welsh Corgi is strong and athletic.

  • Tibetan Mastiff

    Watchful, aloof, imposing, and intimidating: The ancient Tibetan Mastiff is the guardian dog supreme.

  • Shiba Inu

    An ancient Japanese breed, the Shiba Inu is a little but well-muscled dog once employed as a hunter.

  • Poodle

    Despite his diminutive size, the Toy Poodle stands proudly among dogdom's true aristocrats.

  • French Bulldog

    The Frenchie is playful, alert, adaptable, and irresistible, with an even disposition.

  • German Shepherd

    The German Shepherd is a large, agile, dog of noble character and high intelligence.

  • Yorkshire Terrier

    Inside the tiny, adorable Yorkshire Terrier's body is a feisty and confident spirit.

  • Dachschund

    The famously long, low silhouette, ever-alert expression, and vivacious personality makes them a superstar.

  • Cavalier King Charles

    The sweet, gentle, melting expression emanating from large, round eyes is a breed hallmark.
